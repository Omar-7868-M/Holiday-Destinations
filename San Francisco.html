San Francisco

San Francisco

San Francisco known for it cool summers, steep hills, Landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge, cable cars and the former Alcatraz Federal Penitentiary. In 2018 over 26 million tourists visited San Fransico, they estimate by 2030 the number will go up to 31 million.

Cable Cars

Cable Cars

San Francisco's Cable car transport system is the world last manually operated cable car system. One of San Francisco's icons riding in the cable car atleast once is a must there are three main cable cars:Powell-Hyde, Powell-Mason and California Street these are run by San Francisco's Municipal Transportation Agency (SFMTA). For the best experience and view you should take the Powell-Hyde, whilst riding this one you get a view of San Francisco's bay and a View of the famous Alcatraz prison. The Cable cars are a great way to experience the city.
Golden Gate Bridge

Golden Gate Bridge

One of the most well known Landmarks in the world the Golden Gate Bridge, spanning over 1 mile can withstand wind over 100mph. Visiting the Golden Gate Bridge is every visitors #1 place to go. Once named "the bridge that cannot be built", today it is one the seven wonders of the modern world. For the best view the place to go to is Battery Spencer,Fort Baker 19th-century concrete battery featuring panoramic views of Golden Gate & San Francisco.
Steep Hills

Steep Hills

San Francisco has many hills the steepest is Filbert Street which has a maximum gradient of 31.5% giving you a beautiful view of the city. Another place to visit is the twin peaks which gives you a view of the whole city from San Francisco's bay to the skyscrapers to Alcatraz. Photographs just don't do it justice.
